# Travel Guide for Saudia Arabia in July:
- Be prepared for extreme heat in July, with temperatures often exceeding 100°F (38°C).
- Respect local customs and dress modestly, especially when visiting religious sites.
- Plan your activities early in the morning or late in the evening to avoid the strongest sun.
- Stay hydrated and seek shade whenever possible to prevent heat-related illnesses.
- Check the opening hours of attractions and restaurants, as some may have adjusted schedules during Ramadan.
- Familiarize yourself with basic Arabic phrases to facilitate communication with locals.
- Exercise caution and be aware of your surroundings to ensure a safe and enjoyable trip.
